Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle permits or compliance issues?

Yes. We ensure every unit complies with local health, ADA, and environmental standards.

What happens if a porta potty needs servicing during my rental?

Our maintenance crew is on call for cleanings, restocking, and repairs. Simply contact our office, and we’ll respond quickly.

Do you provide cleaning services during long-term rentals?

Yes. Our crews perform scheduled maintenance, cleaning, and restocking to ensure ongoing hygiene.

Can I rent for a long-term project?

Yes, porta-potties can be rented for weeks or months, with flexible maintenance schedules.

Can I rent handwashing stations separately?

Absolutely. We provide standalone stations that can be rented with or without porta-potties.

Absolutely. We offer both standard units and luxury restroom trailers to meet the demands of any type of event.
